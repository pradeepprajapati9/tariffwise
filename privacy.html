Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 July 2026

TariffWise (“we”, “us”) provides a free US import duty and landed-cost calculator, and publishes shopping deals on social platforms. This page explains what we collect and what we do not.

Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or payment details. Everything you type into the calculator is processed in your browser and is never sent to a server we control.

Our host (GitHub Pages) may record standard server logs, such as your IP address and browser type, for security and abuse prevention. We do not have access to those logs in a form that identifies you.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site, and on the social accounts we operate, are Amazon affiliate links. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases, at no extra cost to you. If you click such a link, Amazon may set its own cookies and will handle your data under Amazon’s privacy notice, not this one. We never see what you buy — only aggregate commission totals.

Cookies and analytics

We do not set advertising or tracking cookies of our own. Third-party services you navigate to (such as Amazon or Pinterest) may set their own.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.
